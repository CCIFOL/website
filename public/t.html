Welcome to C.C.I. The Fountain of Life

At C.C.I. The Fountain of Life, we're more than just a community; we're a family united by our faith in Jesus Christ. As you explore our website, we hope you'll discover the warmth, love, and sense of belonging that defines our church.

Our name, "The Fountain of Life," is inspired by the scripture found in Ezekiel 47:9, which says, "And wherever the river goes, every living creature that swarms will live, and there will be very many fish. For this water goes there, that the waters of the sea may become fresh; so everything will live where the river goes." This verse serves as a reminder of our mission to bring life, renewal, and hope to all who come through our doors.

We believe that every person is a beloved child of God, deserving of grace, acceptance, and compassion. Whether you're searching for spiritual fulfillment, seeking answers to life's questions, or simply looking for a place to belong, you're welcome here.
